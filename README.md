## 🚔 About Me

I am a Co-Founder and Chief Strategy Officer of **Cipher Rescue Chains, LLC**, established in 2015 to bridge the gap between private sector tracing and federal seizure operations. Over the past decade, I have simultaneously served in federal law enforcement while building the firm's law enforcement engagement infrastructure.

With 14 years in the U.S. Secret Service (2008–2022) including 7 years in leadership roles, I specialize in international asset recovery, ransomware investigations, and cross-jurisdictional seizure operations.

**Core Expertise:**
- 🚨 Ransomware investigation and cryptocurrency tracing
- 🌍 International asset freezing and seizure operations
- 👮 Law enforcement coordination and task force leadership
- ⚖️ Criminal and civil forfeiture proceedings
- 🕵️ Dark web and cyber fraud investigations
